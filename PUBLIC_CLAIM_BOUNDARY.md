# Public Claim Boundary

Companion documentation added 2026-07-08, after publication; it does not
modify the paper.

## Can Say

- The eight known congruent dissections are reconstructed exactly, with
  symbolic Dehn verification and refinement relations (C1).
- Lagrange forces n | 24 for the known transitive family; full
  Td-invariance only for n ∈ {1,12,24} (C2).
- S6 → S12 refinement is disproved (C3).
- n=16 all-one-face is excluded UNDER the two stated finite MILP
  hypotheses, and unconditionally in the Coxeter-pure instance (C5).
- n=5 is REDUCED, by exact replayable certificates, to one explicit open
  metric residual (C6).

## Must Not Say

- That n=5 congruent dissections are impossible — the paper explicitly
  does not claim this (O1). The only correct public phrasing is the C6
  reduction to the accidental-even metric residual.
- That n=16 all-one-face is excluded unconditionally (O2).
- That n | 24 holds for ALL congruent dissections — the Lagrange
  obstruction is proved for the known transitive family (C2 scope).
- Any n=5 status beyond what `docs/n5_residual_status.md` states — that
  file is this repository's public n=5 frontier.

## Scope Notes

- All certificates are exact rational/symbolic; no numerical-tolerance
  claims are made.
- The Dehn-invariant conventions are those of the paper; use its
  normalization when comparing to other sources.
